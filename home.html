About Web Presence WI

Offering simple and affordable solutions tailored for the small business owner looking to get setup with a basic internet presence.


What is Web Presence?

Web Presence is the use of websites, blogs, email and social media to represent your company.

Prospective customers are likely to check you out online before using your services or coming to your business. Often times, not having a web presence is enough for potential customers to shop elsewhere.
